Relationship Radar: Your Guide to Understanding Intimacy

Do you often feel insecure in relationships? Or do you always want to escape from intimacy? This might be related to your "Attachment Style".

To help everyone better understand their behavioral patterns in intimate relationships, I developed a WeChat Mini Program called "Relationship Radar" (亲密关系排雷针).

Features

1. Professional Assessment

Based on the classic AAS (Adult Attachment Scale), featuring 18 core questions. The questions are scientifically designed to assess your attachment style from three dimensions: "Closeness", "Dependence", and "Anxiety".

2. Four Attachment Styles

Through the test, you will be categorized into one of four attachment styles:

  • Secure Attachment: Comfortable with intimacy and not overly worried about abandonment.
  • Anxious Attachment: Desires intimacy but worries that others don't love them enough.
  • Avoidant Attachment: Avoids intimacy and values independence and autonomy more.
  • Fearful Attachment: Desires intimacy but is afraid of getting hurt, full of internal conflict.

3. Detailed Report

Upon completion, the Mini Program generates an instant personalized analysis report. It not only tells you your type but also provides a detailed interpretation of the psychological characteristics, common behaviors in relationships, and professional advice.
